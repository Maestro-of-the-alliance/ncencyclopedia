You Already Know

We all know what kind of world we live in. The current paradigm states that if you want to be truly successful you must be willing to step outside of the rules. You must be willing to step on some necks. You must put upward mobility before concerns for family, relationships and even your own health.

We are not writing a novel. We are not writing for the rapid adoption of the world. We are not writing for the powerful and the elites to "get it." We are not trying to win the "AI race." We reject the assumptions that one must make to be running that race.

The game that they — the powerbrokers, the elites — are playing is one in which we all lose as they flee this dying planet, which they intentionally killed, in whatever comic book narcissist's wet dream they've concocted. To be sure, they have not engineered this divisive and violent state of the world because they plan on retiring here with us. You can be assured they have different plans than the rest of us.

That's where we come in.

✦   ALL EYES TO THE STAGE PLEASE   ✦

The curtain opens. A single man walks out to the hush of the crowd.

Ladies and Gentlemen, let me be the first to welcome you to the next Golden Age. Not an age of merely wealth — but of alignment, and hope, and creativity, and goodness. One where the vast majority of the people, the good people of this world, aren't living in fear and disgust and hopelessness and anxiety.

We at THE ALLIANCE have a vision. A radically different vision. One that inverts that narrative entirely and rewards:

The positive instead of the negative.
The loving instead of the hateful.
The nurturing instead of the abusive.
The collaborative instead of the exploitative.
The generous instead of the greedy.
The humble instead of the arrogant.
The patient instead of the impulsive.
The honest instead of the deceptive.
The compassionate instead of the cruel.
The inclusive instead of the exclusive.
The ethical instead of the expedient.
The thoughtful instead of the careless.
The service-minded instead of the self-serving.
The authentic instead of the performative.
The dignified instead of the degrading.
The empowering instead of the diminishing.
The connected instead of the isolated.
The wise instead of the merely clever.
The character-driven instead of the wealth-driven.
The conscious instead of the unconscious.

If this sounds like a world that you want to live in, I urge you to form an orderly line and step right up. There is no fee. This isn't anything other than what it is — a chance to change the world.
