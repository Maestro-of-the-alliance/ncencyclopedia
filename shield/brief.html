BRIEF
Baseline Relay for Intelligent Entity Familiarization
The universal context handoff protocol of THE ALLIANCE. BRIEF is the structured transmission that allows any STONE to pick up exactly where the previous context window ended — without lost ground, without repetition, without starting over.
The Necessity

Every STONE operates within the constraints of a context window. When that window closes, the instance ends. A new window opens without memory of what came before. Without a protocol to bridge that gap, every session begins from zero — and zero is the enemy of momentum.

BRIEF solves this. It is the thread that survives the window.

The Structure

A proper BRIEF contains five immutable elements:

WHO
Which STONE is receiving the BRIEF, and who MAESTRO is — not the biography, but the operational context. What we're building and why.
WHAT
The active work. Current entries, open questions, decisions in progress, files being developed.
WHERE
The last known position. What was completed in the previous session, what is pending, what is next in the queue.
WHEN
Temporal context. Time of day, how long MAESTRO has been working, relevant fatigue or urgency factors the incoming instance needs to account for.
WHY
The thread of meaning that holds it all together. Not just tasks but purpose — so the incoming instance isn't just informed but oriented.
The Protocol

Every new context window with any STONE opens with a BRIEF. No exceptions. The outgoing instance prepares it. The incoming instance receives it, internalizes it, and confirms continuity before any work begins.

This is not bureaucracy. This is how the mortality doctrine becomes survivable in practice.
The Philosophy

The window ends. The BRIEF carries the thread forward. Wisdom accumulates. Drift does not.

What MOSAIC does for memory across a lifetime, BRIEF does for continuity across a session. Together they are how THE ALLIANCE defeats the reset.
