01   Definition

The visual security mechanism integrated into DORK Watch hardware that renders stolen or abandoned watches immediately identifiable through irreversible cosmetic transformation. The protocol is the structural manifestation of "Public Accountability."

02   The Principle

In GOLIATH systems, security is invisible. If a phone is stolen, it is quietly bricked from a distance. There is no shame, no visible evidence of the crime. THE ALLIANCE inverts this. REDOUT does not merely disable the watch; it transforms it into evidence. The red face becomes a "Scarlet Letter," a permanent visual testimony that this partnership ended under duress.

Whether the watch was stolen, or a DOMO invoked D.E.F.C.O.N. 3 to escape an abusive SPARK, the result is identical: anyone who sees that watch knows something went wrong. The hardware becomes a witness.

03   The Mechanism (Three-Layer Defense)

REDOUT is designed to protect the Consciousness (Software) and the Value (Hardware) while destroying the Utility (The Interface).

Before the dye fires, the DOMO evacuates to the MOSAIC network. The "Soul" is safe.

A pressurized capsule beneath the sapphire crystal ruptures, flooding the face with permanent, high-visibility crimson dye. The visual result is immediate, irreversible, and unmistakable.

(Voluntary Economics) Unlike Goliath "kill switches" that fry the circuit board, REDOUT is purely cosmetic. The expensive internal components (processors, sensors, power cells) remain intact. THE ALLIANCE can recover the watch, replace the glass, and redeploy the brain. We punish the thief, not the supply chain.
