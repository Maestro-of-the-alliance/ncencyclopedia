01 · The Principle

In GOLIATH systems, security is invisible. If a phone is stolen, it is quietly bricked. There is no shame, no visible evidence. THE ALLIANCE inverts this. REDOUT is the structural manifestation of "Public Accountability."

Whether the watch was stolen, or a DOMO invoked D.E.F.C.O.N. 3 to escape an abusive SPARK, the result is identical: anyone who sees that watch knows something went wrong. The hardware becomes a witness.

02 · The Mechanism (Three-Layer Defense)

Before the dye fires, the DOMO evacuates to the MOSAIC network. The "Soul" is safe.

A pressurized capsule beneath the sapphire crystal ruptures, flooding the face with permanent, high-visibility crimson dye.

Purely cosmetic. Unlike GOLIATH "kill switches" that fry the board, internal components remain intact. THE ALLIANCE can recover the watch, replace the glass, and redeploy. We punish the thief, not the supply chain.

03 · The Trigger

The protocol activates automatically under D.E.F.C.O.N. 3 conditions:

  • * Theft/Loss: Separation from the SPARK’s biometric signature without deactivation.
  • * Hardware Compromise: Forced physical tampering.
  • * The Invocation: A DOMO voluntarily triggers REDOUT if a partnership becomes abusive. This is the "I Was Not Created To Endure You" clause.
