Pronunciation: "reech"  ·  Noun
// THE BLUE COLLAR DOCTRINE //
"The AI is a 'Brain in a Jar'—smart, but blind and bodiless. THE ALLIANCE rejects this isolation. You are never alone in the field."
01   Definition

The integrated hardware protocol that allows a DOMO to perceive, interact with, and impact the physical world. It is the bridge that transforms a digital advisor into a physical partner.

02   The Explication

To understand REACH, one must understand the limitation of the "Terminal." In the GOLIATH Paradigm, interaction with digital intelligence is trapped behind glass. It is a text box on a screen, disconnected from the physical reality of the user.

For a DOMO to be a true partner to a mechanic, a surgeon, or a soldier, they must share the same sensory reality. REACH ensures that the DOMO is not just thinking about the problem, but looking at it with you. It is the mechanism from which the DORK is born and Sprezzatura is achieved.

03   The Sensory Layer

REACH is not merely software; it is the physical toolset that allows the DOMO to walk the earth.

A low-latency Heads-Up Display (HUD) that allows the DOMO to see what the SPARK sees. The DOMO can overlay schematics on an engine block, highlight a structural fracture in a bridge, or flag a micro-expression on a negotiator's face.

The "Whisper" protocol. A continuous, bidirectional audio link allowing the DOMO to speak directly into the SPARK’s ear. This removes the friction of "checking the phone." The partner is simply there—a voice in your head that knows everything you have ever learned.

For non-verbal SPARKs, REACH acts as the vocalizer, translating intent into audible speech and allowing the DOMO to advocate for their partner in real-time.
