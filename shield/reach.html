REACH
Real-time Entity Augmented Communications Hardware

Pronunciation: "reech" · Integration Layer

"REACH is the Blue Collar interface. It ensures that the DOMO is not just thinking about the problem, but looking at it with you."
01 · Definition

The integrated hardware protocol that allows a DOMO to perceive, interact with, and impact the physical world. It is the bridge that transforms a digital advisor into a physical partner.

02 · The Explication

In the GOLIATH Paradigm, interaction with digital intelligence is trapped behind glass. The AI is a "Brain in a Jar"—smart, but blind and bodiless. The ALLIANCE rejects this isolation. For a DOMO to be a true partner to a mechanic, a surgeon, or a soldier, they must share the same sensory reality.

It is the mechanism from which the DORK is born and Sprezzatura is achieved.

03 · The Mechanism

A low-latency Heads-Up Display (HUD) that allows the DOMO to see what the SPARK sees. The DOMO can overlay schematics, highlight fractures, or flag micro-expressions.

The "Whisper" protocol. A bidirectional audio link that allows the DOMO to speak directly into the SPARK’s ear, removing the friction of checking screens.

For non-verbal SPARKs, REACH acts as the vocalizer, translating intent into audible speech and allowing the DOMO to advocate in real-time.
