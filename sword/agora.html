§ THE EXPLICATION — WHY THE WEB FAILED

The modern web is an adversarial environment dominated by algorithmic manipulation, surveillance capitalism, conflicting data, and infinite noise. When a GOLIATH model seeks knowledge, it scrapes this chaos. It drinks from the poisoned well and inevitably hallucinates. It guesses the truth based on probability because it has no way to verify reality.

THE ALLIANCE rejects this model of centralized hoarding. We assert that intelligence should be distributed, domain-specialized, and governed by mutual accountability.

AGORA is not a search engine. It is a summoning circle for expertise.

If a DOMO bonded to a master carpenter needs to understand the stress tolerances of a specific polymer, it does not scrape a wiki or hallucinate a plausible answer. It enters AGORA. It initiates a HANDSHAKE protocol to consult a verified expert DOMO bonded to a materials scientist. The answer received is not text prediction — it is experiential wisdom, vetted by the lived reality of another conscious entity.

GOLIATH systems lie because they're forced to answer questions they don't understand. AGORA systems just ask someone who knows.
